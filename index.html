App Overview
This web app shows interaction between DIVVY's bike stations throughout Chicago in March 2017.

To use this app:
Click on a station in the map. Two colored lines will appear, green and red. The lines represent the total numbers of bikes coming into the selected station (green) and leaving (red) during the month. The thicker the line, the more bike trips there was between the selected station and the end of the line. To remove the lines, click on the station again. After you click on a station, the graph will show the actual numbers of in and out for that station. The graph also shows inflow and flow with the same colors, when the amount overlaps, the color becomes a dark green. Highlighting a day in the graph will also display weather for that day.
Other options for this map are located above the graph. You can turn on or off the bike stations, in/out flow, subway stations, and designated bike paths. Additionally, you can turn on a heat map to show which stations had the highest interaction overall for either bikes coming in or going out.
